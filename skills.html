This is the latest website that I have worked on. The website is a short summary look at the city of Calgary and its settlement history. For this website, I explored using bootstrap; using the grids, the different photo shapes and also sliding banner.

A site made for a group project, the conent of the website are talking about adhering to indigenous voices in regards to pipelines and includes the negative affects of ruptured pipelines. With this website I ensured that the site was accessible through adding a tab button to go to the main subject, the contrast level between the background and text color met WCAG level AA.
